Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour
& Royalty

Royal Platter was born from a simple belief — that great food is an act of love. We blend time-honoured family recipes with the finest locally-sourced ingredients to create an experience that goes far beyond a meal.

From our signature slow-cooked biryanis to our melt-in-your-mouth kebabs, every dish is crafted with intention, care, and a generous pinch of passion.
